Welcome!


Welcome to Max’s Review Corner, where I will review anything and everything that is suggested by my readers.
I especially have a passion for movies and shows, and hope to make some myself one day.

What do I review?
I review movies, shows, games, and basically anything else that’s sent in through the suggestions form by my readers.
How are reviews rated?
The media that I review will be rated with a MRC (pronounced Mercy) rating out of 100.
How do I submit a suggestion to review something I like?
You can submit a suggestion directly to my email via the suggestions tab on the left side of this page, by clicking this link, or by sending a message to my email found at the bottom of the page.
